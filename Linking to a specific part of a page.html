King of comedy

This talented actor started his journey by doing minor roles in TV series. In 1997, he became a hot topic with the success of “God Of Cookery.” He gained a lot of popularity and became one of the highest paid actors of the Chinese entertainment industry

He has acted and produced many movies and TV shows like Kung Fu, Shaolin Soccer, King of Comedy, CJ7, The Mermaid, Royal Ramp, The Justice of Life, The Final Combat and so on

Fact

His childhood was comparatively gentle,and he was fascinated by BruceLee. In 1982, after graduating high school, Stephen and his friend Tony Leung Chui-Wai auditioned for TVB acting school, but Stephen didn’t make the cut

Awards

Asia Pacific Film Festival Best Actor award for Justice, My Foot in 1992. Blue Ribbon Awards Best Foreign Language Film for Kung Fu Hustle in 2005. Florida Film Critics Circle Awards Best Foreign Language Film for Kung Fu Hustle.

Amsterdam Fantastic Film Festival Silver Scream Award for Kung Fu Hustle in 2005.
